    For what value of \(c\) does the polynomial \(p(t)=1+ct+4t^2\) belong to the linear span of polynomials \(p_1(t)=1+t\), \(p_2(t)=t+t^2\).

    The correct answer is: 5

    Feedback

    The correct answer is: -2
